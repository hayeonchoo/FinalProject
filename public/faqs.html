What is the purpose of the website?

Recipes for Society is aimed at mitigating the overload of information when searching for a specific recipe from a wide range of options. More can be found on our

About Us page.

Where do you get the recipes from?

We are utilizing the Spoonacular API which provides us with the recipes and all information regarding them.

Is it free to use?

Yes, Recipes for Society is completely free to use, as well as the Spoonacular API. You can register for a Spoonacular account for free and redeem a key

here.
