While food is essential to all human beings, nutritional needs are unique to each individual. With the millions of recipes in existence it is easy for someone to suffer from information overload trying to find something new that fits their demands. Whether it is due to a busy schedule or food intolerances, many fall into the habit of cooking the same meals repeatedly. We set out to solve this problem by creating Recipes for Society. Our platform allows you to search recipes based on a variety of parameters that are applicable to you and your specific needs.
