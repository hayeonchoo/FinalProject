How to use the home page

The home page is where you can find recipes, by inputting a keyword into the search bar to filter and display results according to your search criteria. The results will display with an image, name of the recipe, time needed to cook and ingredients list for each item.

How to use the Favorites Page

Feel free to "heart" as many recipes as you want. This will add the recipe to your favorites page, allowing you to bookmark specific recipes you would like to come back to or save for future use.

Find a recipe and start cooking!

The recipes and relevant information are all organized and stored according to a variety of nutritional attributes by Spoonacular engineers using their complex food ontology. All recipes have been analyzed and attributed to specific categories such as certain allergies, food intolerances and dietary lifestyles.
